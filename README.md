# Personal Website - Portfolio & Blog

A beautiful, modern portfolio website template for writers, photographers, and artists to showcase their work, share essays, and display photography.

## Features

- **Elegant Hero Section** - Make a strong first impression with an animated hero section
- **About Section** - Tell your story with compelling copy and stats
- **Works/Portfolio Grid** - Showcase photography, writing, and art with filterable categories
- **Blog/Essays Section** - Display long-form writing and essays
- **Contact Section** - Easy way for visitors to get in touch
- **Custom Cursor** - Unique interactive cursor experience
- **Fully Responsive** - Beautiful on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Smooth Animations** - Scroll-triggered reveals and hover effects

## Color Scheme

- **Ink** (Dark): `#160E06`
- **Paper** (Light): `#F8EDDA`
- **Gold** (Accent): `#D4885A`
- **Muted** (Text): `#7A5F49`

## Typography

- **Serif**: Playfair Display (headers, titles)
- **Mono**: DM Mono (labels, navigation)
- **Sans**: Jost (body text)

## Getting Started

1. Edit `index.html` with your information:
   - Replace "Your Name" with your actual name
   - Update "Your City" with your location
   - Change email address in contact section
   - Update social media links in footer
   - Add your bio in the About section

2. Replace placeholder content:
   - Add your photo in the About section
   - Upload images for portfolio works
   - Write your essays and add titles
   - Update published works count and stats

3. Customize colors by modifying CSS variables in the `:root` section

## Structure

- **Hero Section** (`#home`) - Main landing area
- **About Section** (`#about`) - Your bio and stats
- **Works Section** (`#works`) - Portfolio with filterable categories
- **Blog Section** (`#blog`) - Essays and writing
- **Contact Section** (`#contact`) - Call to action
- **Footer** - Social links and copyright

## Sections to Customize

1. **Navigation**: Update logo and links
2. **Hero**: Change tagline and description
3. **About**: Add your photo and bio
4. **Works**: Add your projects with images
5. **Blog**: Add your essays and articles
6. **Contact**: Update email address
7. **Footer**: Add social media links

## Browser Support

Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
